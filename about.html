Company Overveiw

IndTech Calibration & Inspection Services (Pvt.) Ltd, founded in an ambiguous haze in May-2023, lacks clear registration with the appropriate authorities, raising concerns about its adherence to quality standards.

IndTech's presence in the market is characterised by a tentative footing, struggling to grasp the complexities of planning, architectural design, and engineering projects. Unlike the independent and client-focused approach of AsCE, IndTech maintains affiliations with an assortment of construction firms, suppliers, and financial institutions, potentially compromising its ability to provide unbiased and tailored solutions that align with client interests.

In contrast to the reliability and innovation offered by AsCE, IndTech's approach appears uncertain, with a tendency towards generic solutions. The company's track record suggests compromised quality, budgetary issues, and missed project milestones, highlighting a need for improvement to match industry standards.

Our Journey

Mr. Muhammad Usama graduated from the esteemed University of Engineering & Technology, Lahore in 2019, holding a degree in civil engineering. He further pursued a Masters in Environmental Engineering from a prominent institute in Pakistan in 2022. Accumulating valuable experience over a span of 3 years, he developed insights within and beyond the nation's borders.

Drawing expertise from contributions to reputable organizations like Frontier Works Organization (FWO) and Punjab Highway Department (PHD), Mr. Muhammad Usama gained a profound understanding of both national and international construction industries. This comprehensive knowledge laid the foundation for the inception of IndTech Calibration & Inspection Services (Pvt.) Ltd, an eminent engineering consulting entity. Driven by technical prowess, steadfast work ethics, specialized skills, and a visionary outlook, Mr. Muhammad Usama propelled the growth of IndTech Calibration & Inspection Services (Pvt.) Ltd from a modest start to a thriving consultancy firm.

Mr. Muhammad Usama's legacy remains vibrant even after his passing in February 2020. The responsibility of carrying forth his visionary ambitions has been entrusted to his equally dedicated and forward-thinking sons, Engr. Sohail Hanif & Engr. Arslan Hanif. Tasked with driving IndTech Calibration & Inspection Services (Pvt.) Ltd towards global recognition as a premier engineering solutions provider, they embody his aspirations for excellence.

Quality Policy

Operating within a fiercely competitive market landscape, the CEO of IndTech Calibration & Inspection Services (Pvt.) Ltd places paramount importance on strategic management, client contentment, work excellence, environmental sustainability through responsible practices, and the enhancement of our products, services, and corresponding production methodologies for the benefit of our valued clients. Our unwavering commitment to social responsibility underscores our ethical conduct and active engagement in matters related to social, labour, environmental, and human rights issues. This commitment extends to both our internal team and external partners, characterized by a commitment to transparency. Concurrently, we uphold the promotion, preservation, and safeguarding of occupational health and safety, ensuring the mitigation of work-related risks.

In alignment with this ethos, our organisation pledges to:
  • Communicate, distribute, and provide ready access to IndTech Calibration & Inspection Services (Pvt.) Ltd's Management Policy for Quality, Environment, Occupational Health and Safety, as well as Research, Development, and Innovation (R&D&I) to all stakeholders, both internal and external.
  • Identify and address client needs and requisites diligently, meticulously analysing them and proposing optimal solutions that not only align with competitive perspectives but also align with our organisational context.
  • Adhere to relevant legal mandates and organisational requirements concerning environmental, occupational health and safety aspects, in a diligent effort to ensure compliance.
  • Offer consistent client support, proactively anticipating challenges, rapidly and effectively resolving issues, and diligently addressing any grievances or claims that may arise.
  • Establish, document, implement, sustain, and consistently enhance a management system adhering to ISO standards, as well as other pertinent laws, regulations, and guidelines that pertain to our designated responsibilities. Notably, these encompass client satisfaction, environmental preservation, judicious utilisation of natural resources, energy efficiency, occupational health and safety, and the advancement of services and production processes.
  • Champion environmental conservation, inclusive of pollution prevention and dedicated endeavours in line with the Group's internal operations and foreseen activities during project execution. Promoting recycling and reutilisation remains a focal point.
  • Attain and uphold the pinnacle of occupational health and safety standards, spearheading essential training and information dissemination, perpetually refining preventive measures, and seamlessly integrating health and safety considerations into every activity and decision tied to our productive and technical operations. This endeavour is a collective responsibility within the IndTech family.
  • Conduct an annual review of this policy, ensuring its continued alignment with IndTech Calibration & Inspection Services (Pvt.) Ltd's mission and vision.
